FAQs

How do I schedule an appointment?
To schedule an appointment, please call our appointments desk at +233550611317 or use our online appointment scheduling system.
What should I do in case of a medical emergency?
In case of a medical emergency, please call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.
What are your visiting hours?
Our visiting hours are from 08:00 - 17:00. However, exceptions may apply based on patient condition and unit policies. Please check with our staff for specific visiting hour guidelines.
How do I obtain medical records?
To request medical records, please fill out the appropriate form available on our website or contact our medical records department directly. Additional documentation and fees may be required.
Do you offer telemedicine appointments?
Yes, we offer telemedicine appointments for certain medical services. Please contact our appointments desk to inquire about telemedicine availability and scheduling.
What insurance plans do you accept?
We accept a wide range of insurance plans. Please check our website or contact our billing department for a list of accepted insurance providers.
